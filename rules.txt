1. Get to the very top by jumping through the platforms and solving each question
2. Questions are randomized per topic, but topics are set to a certain order
3. Questions will reappear when you jump/fall on a platform
4. Math topics are algebra, geometry, statistics, trigonometry, and calculus
5. Science topics are biology, earth science, environmental science, chemistry, and physics
6. Controls start at up, left, and right arrow keys but can be changed through settings
7. Avatar design can be chosen through customize
8. You will lose if you run out of lives from incorrect answer choices or take more than 5 minutes
9. To pause, use the escape key (this is set and cannot be changed through the game)
10. Good luck and enjoy!!!
